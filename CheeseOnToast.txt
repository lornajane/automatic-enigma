Cheese on Toast

Ingredients
2 slices of bread
6 slices of cheese
Butter

You wil need
A toaster
A grill

Optional
Worcestershire sauce

Steps
1. Toast bread.
2. Butter toast.
3. Put cheese slices on toast, 3 slices per piece of toast.
4. Optionally add Worcestershire sauce on top of cheese slices
5. Place under grill until cheese has melted
